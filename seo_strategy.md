# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing and artist pages for the Luis R Conriquez official site
- Homepage (`/`) and all on-page public sections (`/#musica`, `/#reconocimientos`, `/#bts`, `/#suscribete`)
- Tour dates page (`/shows`)
- Public static assets that affect crawling, sharing, and AI visibility (`robots.txt`, sitemap, OG image, favicon, llms.txt)

## Out of scope
- Authenticated dashboards
- Admin pages
- Internal APIs
- Design-only concerns that do not affect search visibility, social sharing, or crawler access

## Target audience
- Spanish-speaking fans searching for official music, tour dates, videos, and artist information about Luis R Conriquez

## Primary keywords
- luis r conriquez
- luis r conriquez sitio oficial
- luis r conriquez shows
- luis r conriquez boletos
- corridos bélicos
